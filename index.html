Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical session last?

Most sessions last 30-45 minutes, allowing time for multiple poses, outfit changes, and real-time image review. We ensure you never feel rushed and get the perfect shot.

What should I wear to my session?

We recommend solid colors and professional attire that matches your industry. Bring 2-3 outfit options, and we'll help you choose what photographs best.

How quickly will I receive my photos?

You'll receive your fully retouched images within 3-5 business days. Rush delivery is available for an additional fee if needed sooner.

Do you provide hair and makeup services?

Yes, we can arrange professional hair and makeup services for an additional fee. Please request this service when booking your session.

What is your rescheduling policy?

We understand things come up. You can reschedule your session with 48 hours notice at no additional charge. Last-minute changes may incur a fee.
